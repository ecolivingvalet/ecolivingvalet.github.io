Eco Living Valet Services

Step 1

Valet Living offers community collection and service times. Check with your community management team to verify collection dates, and service times at your community.

Step 2

Place the Garbage Bag(s) at the designated area. Check with your community management team to verify where the designated area is located and waste collection dates and service times at your community.

Step 3

Ensure garbage has been collected by your community management team by 9:30 PM. If you will not be home during collection times, please contact your community management team to organize a special pick up time.

Residential Services


Trash: (Apartment residents only)

  • 2 Bag Maximum - Each Bag Secured, Not Torn or Leaking
  • 25 lbs Maximum Per Bag

Recyclables: (Apartment and Single Family Residents)

  • Glass must be separated from the rest of recycling and should be placed in cardboard box or bag
  • Cardboard needs to be broken down
  • 25lbs max per bag of recyclables
  • 2 bags maximum

Recycling Items Accepted:

  • Plastic
  • Aluminum / Tin
  • Paper/Magazines/Newspaper
  • Cardboard (boxes broken down)
  • Glass (clear, brown, green)


Tips for preparing your trash and recycling below.


Trash tips Recycling tips

Trash tips


“Contact your community manager if you have bulk items such as furniture pieces,
or boxes of old kitchen & household items, etc.”

  • No loose trash.

  • Double-bag all pet waste.

  • No broken glass or sharp objects.

  • No oversized items (e.g. furniture, large boxes, etc.)
Recycling Tips

“Recycling participation is a challenge for many properties, so EcoLiving implemented a program that makes it easy for every resident to participate in and start living a greener lifestyle.”

Placing non-recyclable materials contaminates all recyclables rendering them non-recyclable.
